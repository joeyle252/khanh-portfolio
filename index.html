Soft skills

Soft skills are character traits, personal attributes, and other non-technical abilities that help you work and communicate with other people.

Teamwork

Teamwork is the collaborative effort of a group to achieve a common goal or to complete a task in the most effective and efficient way

  • Open communication to avoid conflicts.
  • Effective coordination to avoid confusion
  • Efficient cooperation to perform the tasks in a timely manner adn produce the required results.

Communication skill

Communication skills are abilities you use when giving and receiving different kinds of information. They involve listening, speaking, observing and empathizing.

  • Active listen: paying close attention to the person who is speaking to.
  • Motivation: can communicate and inspire team members to work productively and meet established goals.
